When creating or renaming Jira issues (BSP-###, SKRP-###, …) and Confluence pages, write the title so it is simple, intuitive, and in English — not Korean, not a long descriptive sentence.

**Why:** English titles are searchable and scannable by the whole team; a short intuitive title conveys intent at a glance and keeps the tracker consistent.

**How to apply:** produce a concise English title that captures the core intent; avoid Korean, jargon dumps, and full-sentence titles. Put detail in the body/description, not the title. Body language can be whatever the task calls for — this rule is about the title.
